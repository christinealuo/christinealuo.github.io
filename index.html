About

Welcome to my website! My name is Christine and I'm currently a junior studying computer science at UC Berkeley. My favorite class this semester is the Web Design DeCal! I'm currently taking CS 162 and CS 186. I'm also taking Korean 1A and it's pretty fun learning a new language. Outside of school, I enjoy teaching as a junior mentor for Computer Science Mentors, a club that provides small group tutoring sections for lower-division computer science classes here at Berkeley. There I help teach CS 61A and CS 88. I also enjoy watching Terrace House, playing Disney Tsum Tsum, and napping, Thanks for visiting my website!
